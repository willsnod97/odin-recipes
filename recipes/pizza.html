Pizza

Description

This is a classic pizza recipe featuring a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and a generous topping of cheese and pepperoni, baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly, about 12-15 minutes.
