Lasagna

Description

This is a traditional lasagna recipe featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat until beef is browned and onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce and simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes.
